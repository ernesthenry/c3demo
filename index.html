Why We Exist?

As a regional movement, C3 Alive works to establish and maintain strong oversight relationships, develop and strengthen our C3 brand and resources, as well as train and equip our leaders through regional conferences and gatherings. The C3 Alive Team provides support to the region through successful assimilation of new churches and ministers into our movement, providing additional pastoral care and support to our Senior Pastors and developing effective strategies to assist with process and policy compliance. C3 Alive reflects the vision and mission of C3 Global, to save the lost, make disciples and build the church.
